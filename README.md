# 🇮🇳 JanVoice AI – AI-Powered Governance Intelligence Platform

> **Google Build with AI – Code for Communities Hackathon 2026**

JanVoice AI is an AI-powered governance platform that enables citizens to report public issues through voice, text, images, videos, and location while helping Members of Parliament (MPs) and government departments prioritize, analyze, and resolve complaints using Artificial Intelligence.

---

## 👥 Team

**Team Name:** Civic Mind

## 📌 Problem Statement

Government representatives receive thousands of citizen complaints through multiple channels such as phone calls, emails, social media, and offline offices.

Current challenges include:

- No unified complaint platform
- Duplicate complaints
- Fake or spam reports
- Manual complaint categorization
- Lack of prioritization
- Delayed issue resolution
- Limited multilingual accessibility
- No AI-assisted decision making
- Poor constituency analytics
- Lack of transparency

These problems make it difficult for MPs and government departments to identify and resolve high-impact issues efficiently.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

JanVoice AI provides a unified AI-powered governance platform where citizens can report problems in multiple formats, while AI automatically understands, verifies, categorizes, prioritizes, and routes each complaint to the appropriate authority.

The platform also provides MPs with real-time constituency insights, analytics, AI recommendations, and interactive dashboards for informed decision-making.

# 📊 Priority Score

The platform calculates complaint priority using:

```
Priority Score =
Verification Score
× Urgency Score
× Impact Score
× Feasibility Score
```

This helps government officials focus on the most critical issues first.
